Popular RECEIPES

1.Salmon Burgers

Step 1

In a large bowl, combine salmon with egg, bread crumbs, garlic, lemon zest and juice, dill, Dijon, Worcestershire, and red pepper flakes. Season with salt and pepper and stir until fully combined, then form into 3 or 4 patties.

Step 2

In a large skillet, heat oil. Add patties and cook until golden, about 4 minutes on each side.

Step 3

Serve patties on buns with mayonnaise, romaine, tomatoes, and onions.

2.Corn Salad

Step 1

In a large bowl, combine the cooked corn kernels, diced tomato, diced red onion, and chopped cilantro.

Step 2

In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lime juice, salt, and pepper.

Step 3

Pour the dressing over the corn salad and toss until well combined. Serve immediately or chill in the refrigerator until ready to serve.

3.Paner Kathi Role

Step 1

Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds. Once the seeds start to splutter, add sliced onions and sauté until they turn translucent.

Step 2

Add sliced capsicum and sauté for 2-3 minutes until they are slightly soft. Add coriander powder, turmeric powder, red chili powder and salt, and mix well.

Step 3

Add paneer strips to the pan and toss well with the onion-capsicum masala until the paneer is coated well. Cook for another 2-3 minutes.

Step 4

Heat the roti or paratha on a tawa or griddle until they are slightly crisp and warm.

Step 5

Spread 1 tablespoon of mint chutney and 1 tablespoon of tamarind chutney on each roti or paratha.

Step 6

Place 1-2 spoonfuls of the paneer mixture on one side of the roti or paratha and roll it up tightly.

Step 7

Serve the paneer kathi rolls hot with extra mint chutney and tamarind chutney on the side.

4.Pav Bhaji

Step 1

In a pan, heat the butter and add chopped onions. Fry till they turn translucent. Then add chopped tomatoes and cook till they become soft and mushy.

Step 2

Add the mixed vegetables to the pan and cook for 5-7 minutes. Add mashed potatoes and mix well. Add pav bhaji masala, red chili powder, and salt. Mix everything together and cook for another 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally.

Step 3

In another pan, heat some butter and lightly toast the pav buns.

Step 4

Serve the pav bhaji hot with a dollop of butter, chopped onion, and a squeeze of lemon juice. Enjoy with the toasted pav buns.

5.Chickpea Curry

In a pot, sauté onions, garlic, and ginger in oil until softened. Add chickpeas, canned tomatoes, coconut milk, and spices. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer until the sauce is thickened and the chickpeas are tender, about 20-30 minutes. Serve with rice.

6.Palak Paneer

In a large pan, sauté chopped onions and garlic until translucent. Add chopped spinach and cook until wilted. Stir in cubed paneer cheese, tomato puree, and a spice mixture of garam masala, cumin, and coriander. Simmer for 10-15 minutes, then serve with rice or naan bread.
